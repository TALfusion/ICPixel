## 1. Nature of the Service

ICPixel is a **fully decentralized application** running entirely on the Internet Computer Protocol (ICP) blockchain. There are no centralized servers, databases, or cloud infrastructure operated by us. All game data, including pixel placements, alliance information, and transaction records, is stored on-chain in ICP canisters.

**You acknowledge and understand that:**
- All actions you take on the blockchain are **permanent, public, and immutable**
- We do not control the Internet Computer blockchain or its infrastructure

- Blockchain data is inherently transparent and accessible to anyone

## 2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do **not** operate any off-chain servers, databases, analytics platforms, or tracking infrastructure.

## 3. Blockchain Data

When you interact with ICPixel, the following data is recorded **directly on the ICP blockchain** by the protocol itself, not by us:

- Your **Internet Identity (II) principal** — a pseudonymous cryptographic identifier
- **Pixel placements** — coordinates, color, and timestamp
- **Alliance membership** — join/leave events
- **Transaction records** — ICRC-2 payment approvals and transfers
- **NFT ownership** — minting, transfers, and burns

This data is:
- **Public by nature** — anyone can read blockchain state
- **Not controlled by us** — we cannot delete, modify, or restrict access to on-chain data
- **Pseudonymous** — your principal is not linked to your real-world identity unless you choose to make that connection yourself
- **Permanent** — blockchain data persists independently of our involvement

## 4. Internet Identity

Authentication is handled entirely by **Internet Identity (II)**, a decentralized identity service operated by the DFINITY Foundation. We do not:
- Have access to your II credentials, biometrics, passkeys, or recovery mechanisms
- Store any authentication tokens or session data
- Have the ability to link your II principal to your real-world identity

Your relationship with Internet Identity is governed by DFINITY's own terms and privacy policy. We are not a party to that relationship.

## 5. ICP Ledger and Financial Transactions

All financial transactions (pixel purchases, alliance creation fees, reward claims) are processed through the **ICP Ledger Canister** and **ICRC-2 standard**, which are decentralized protocols on the Internet Computer.

**You acknowledge that:**
- We do not custody, hold, or control your ICP tokens at any time
- Payments are made via ICRC-2 approve/transfer_from — you explicitly authorize each transaction
- We cannot reverse, refund, or modify any blockchain transaction once confirmed
- Transaction fees are set by the ICP protocol, not by us
- The value of ICP tokens and any game assets may fluctuate and may become worthless
- All transfers are final and irreversible
