# How to Play

## Getting Started

1. Open ICPixel in your browser
2. Click **Sign In** — you'll be redirected to Internet Identity
3. Create or use an existing Internet Identity anchor
4. You're in. The map is live.

## Placing Pixels

- Click anywhere on the map to place a pixel with your selected color
- Pick colors from the palette at the bottom of the screen
- Press **1-9** to quickly switch between colors
- Hold **Tab** and hover over the map to pick a color from it (eyedropper)
- There's a **10-second cooldown** between placements
- You can overwrite anyone's pixel — and they can overwrite yours

## Buying Pixels

Each pixel costs **$0.05** in ICP (converted at the current exchange rate).

1. Click the **PIXEL** counter in the nav bar, or the **+** button next to it
2. Choose how many pixels to buy
3. Approve the ICRC-2 payment from your wallet
4. Your pixel balance increases — spend them by clicking on the map

## Viewing the Map

- **Scroll** to zoom in/out
- **Drag** to pan around
- **Arrow keys** or **WASD** to move the camera
- The map auto-fits when it grows to a new stage

## Spectator Mode

You can view the map, watch the replay, and browse alliances without signing in. You just can't place pixels or earn rewards.
