# Overview

ICPixel is a collaborative pixel battle game running entirely on the Internet Computer blockchain. Every pixel, every alliance, every NFT — fully on-chain.

## The Concept

Players place colored pixels on a shared canvas. The map starts tiny (1x1) and grows as players fill it. Form alliances, claim territory, complete pixel-art missions, and earn real ICP rewards.

Think r/place — but with real money, real stakes, and real rewards.

## Core Loop

1. **Place pixels** — Pick a color, click on the map. Each pixel costs $0.05 in ICP.
2. **Join an alliance** — Team up with other players to tackle missions.
3. **Complete missions** — Paint a specific image on the map. When 95% matches, the alliance leader receives an NFT.
4. **Earn rewards** — Every correct pixel you place in a mission earns you a share of the reward pool. Paid out in ICP.
5. **Grow the map** — As players fill the canvas, it expands through stages until the season ends.

## Key Features

- **Fully on-chain** — Frontend, backend, and all game data hosted on ICP canisters. No servers, no databases, no cloud.
- **Real money** — Pixels cost ICP. Rewards are paid in ICP. NFTs are real ICRC-7 tokens.
- **Seasonal** — Each season has a fresh map. NFT holders from completed seasons receive treasury distributions.
- **Open warfare** — Anyone can overwrite anyone's pixel. Alliances must defend their territory.
- **No bots** — Internet Identity login required. Economic barriers make botting unprofitable.

## What Makes It Different

Unlike r/place (temporary, free, no stakes), ICPixel adds:
- **Economic incentive** — Correct pixels in missions earn you money
- **Permanent artifacts** — Completed missions become NFTs with on-chain metadata and images
- **Alliance dynamics** — Coordinated teams vs solo players vs saboteurs
- **Seasonal scarcity** — Season 1 NFTs will never be minted again
