# Map & Seasons

## Map Growth

The map starts at **1x1** pixel and grows through stages as players fill it. A stage advances when **95% of all cells** have been painted at least once.

### Stages

| Stage | Map Size | Total Cells |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| 0 | 1x1 | 1 |
| 1 | 5x5 | 25 |
| 2 | 10x10 | 100 |
| 3 | 50x50 | 2,500 |
| 4 | 100x100 | 10,000 |
| 5 | 500x500 | 250,000 |

When the map grows, it expands equally in all directions. Existing pixels stay in place — new white space appears around the edges.

## Seasons

A season is one complete lifecycle of the map — from 1x1 to final stage.

### Timeline

1. **Season starts** — Fresh 1x1 map. First pixel triggers growth to 5x5.
2. **Growth phase** — Players fill stages one by one. Alliances form, missions begin.
3. **Final stage reached** — When 500x500 is achieved, a **7-day countdown** starts.
4. **Season ends** — No more pixels can be placed. Remaining treasury is distributed to NFT holders.
5. **New season** — Fresh map, fresh alliances, new opportunities.

### What Carries Over

- **NFTs** — Yours forever, across all seasons
- **Token balances** — ICP stays in your wallet

### What Resets

- The map — blank canvas
- Alliances and missions — start fresh
- Pixel credits — unused credits carry over (you paid for them)

### Season Numbering

Seasons are numbered starting from 1. Earlier seasons are inherently more valuable — Season 1 NFTs can never be recreated.

## Default Colors

The map starts as a white canvas. Every cell begins as white (#FFFFFF) until a player paints it.
